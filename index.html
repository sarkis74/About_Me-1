About H'Liana

Hi! My name is H'Liana. I'm a 25-year old interior architect in Seattle, WA. I have a passion for building things that can affect people in positive ways -- to inspire, to heal, and to learn. I graduated from Winthrop University with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Interior Design in 2012, and have since designed a variety of different project types including senior living facilities, bars and restaurants, theme park rides, and tech workplaces up to 500,000 square feet.

Interests

My interests include design and architecture, photography, playing [non-competitive] video games, going to concerts, and exploring the outdoors with my mini Australian Shepherd, Kiba.
